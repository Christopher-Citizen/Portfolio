2023. 8m30s. Video8, Super8 and Archival material

Working the Land Again explores land expropriation, environmental pollution, and worker subjectification in post-colonial Aotearoa New Zealand. The film weaves together original images of contemporary dairy farming practice, aerial cinematography produced by the state, and early British paintings of the land.
